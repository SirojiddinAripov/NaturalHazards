In this website you can explore a few natural disasters sorted by their main element. For example if you wish to know more about volcanoes hit the {🔥Fire} button on the header.

If you wish to adjust the font sizes press the red plus and minus buttons on the top right corner.

What can you expect?

Each page about natural hazards will include

  1. Picture of where that Hazard is most common.
  2. Pictures of the local population in that area.

As well as these questions and answers.

  1. What causes that natural hazard?
  2. How dangerous is that natural hazard?
  3. What can you do to prepare?

Links to official government websites will be given if you wish to learn more about safety protocols.
